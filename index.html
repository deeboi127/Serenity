My Dearest Caca,

My world is filled with thoughts of you,
In everything I say and do.
And though you're far, I feel you near,
And wish that you were truly here.

I miss the light within your eyes,
Beneath these wide and lonely skies.
I miss your head upon my shoulder,
My world without you feels much colder.

You're more than just a simple part,
You are the home within my heart.
The sanctuary I long to find,
The only peace within my mind.

So know this love is deep and true,
My world begins and ends with you.
And I will wait this absence through,
To find my greatest joy anew.
